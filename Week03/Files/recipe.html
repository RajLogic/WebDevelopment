French Toast

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Beat together egg, milk, salt, spices and vanilla
  2. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides
  4. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden
  5. Serve hot
